Orbit Cache is a slim infrastructure layer for retrieval-heavy LLM products. It keeps your current model stack intact, but moves hot context, ranking artifacts, and repeated search paths into a fast cache so the product feels measurably faster on day one.

Why this launch matters

Teams have already paid the model bill. What they still have not fixed is the user-perceived lag between question and answer.

  • Knowledge search workload: retrieval spend dropped 31% once repeated passages stopped re-querying the vector store.
  • Customer support copilot: 42% faster median response time after hot-ticket caching.
  • Internal research assistant: prompt iteration volume tripled because the latency ceiling stopped shaping the workflow.

Proof from the pilot

Orbit Cache solves that narrow problem first: faster repeated retrieval, fewer wasted vector calls, and more room to experiment on prompts.
