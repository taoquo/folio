Proposal to establish a monthly agent review council before the next deployment wave.
Dear Board Members,

I recommend establishing a monthly agent review council beginning in May so deployment, risk, and operating decisions happen in one room before the next scale step.

Our strongest gains now come from agents that cross product, platform, and support boundaries, yet we still review those systems in separate tracks. The result is delayed learning; the cost of fragmented review is now operational, not theoretical.

I request approval for a 90-day pilot with product, platform, support, and security representatives. If approved this week, the first session can take place on May 12.

Thank you for considering the proposal. I believe this is the lightest process change that still matches the systems we now operate.
